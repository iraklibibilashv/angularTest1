✦ Our Story

Passion on Every Plate

Born from a love of Georgian culture and cuisine, Savoria brings ancient recipes to life with a modern touch.

Est. 2019 · Tbilisi

Where Tradition Meets Craft

Savoria was founded in 2019 by chef Nino Lomidze, who grew up cooking alongside her grandmother in the Kakheti region. Her dream was simple — to share the warmth of Georgian hospitality with the world, one dish at a time.

Every recipe on our menu has a story. From our slow-braised Chakapuli to our stone-baked Khachapuri, we source ingredients locally and prepare everything fresh each morning.

Today, Savoria is home to a team of 12 passionate chefs and over 80 dishes that celebrate Georgia's rich culinary heritage.
